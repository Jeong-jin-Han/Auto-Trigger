How to use Auto Trigger
1
Record a click pattern
Click Record, then click any elements on the page you want to automate. Click Stop when done. Use Resume to add more clicks. The trash icon clears all recorded clicks.
2
Run with Auto Trigger (video detection)
Auto Trigger
Watches the current tab for a video to end, then automatically fires your recorded pattern. Works on YouTube, YouTube Shorts, and most HTML5 video sites. Does not work on livestreams or Flash-based players.
3
Run with Macro (manual repeat)
Macro
Replays your recorded pattern N times with a configurable delay between each repeat. Use this for any repetitive task on any website — form filling, button clicking, navigation, etc.
☰
Header buttons
Theme
Toggles between dark and light mode. Follows your system preference by default.
Sound
Toggles sound alert. Hover the bell to reveal the volume slider.
Refresh
Refreshes the page and resets the extension. Recorded clicks will be cleared.
DEV
Shows or hides the developer log and recorded click details.
