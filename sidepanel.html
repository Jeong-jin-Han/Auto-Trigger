Record a click pattern
Click Record, perform your clicks on the page, then click Stop. Your clicks are saved as a reusable pattern.
2
Choose how to run it
Macro
Replay the recorded pattern manually a set number of times. Works on any site for any repetitive action.
Auto Trigger
Automatically fires the pattern when a video ends. Keeps watching until you stop it.
